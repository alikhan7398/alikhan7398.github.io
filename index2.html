SUBHASH CHANDRA BOSE

Introduction

Subhas Chandra Bose was an Indian nationalist whose defiant patriotism made him a hero in India,
but whose attempts during World War II to rid India of British rule with the help of Nazi Germany and Imperial Japan left a troubled legacy

Profile Photo

About

  1. Subhas Bose was born into wealth and privilege in a large Bengali
    family in Orissa during the high noon of the British Raj.
  2. Sent to England at his father's urging to take the Indian Civil Service examination
  3. In 1921 to join the nationalist movement led by Mahatma Gandhi and the Indian National Congress, Bose at first worked with C. R. Das in Bengal
  4. Bose rose precociously to become Congress president in 1938
  5. In April 1941, Bose arrived in Nazi Germany
  6. He created Azad Hind Fauj on july 4th, 1943

Timeline
